# The Boss Button Explained

Sometimes you just need to cancel everything. No explanations, no back-and-forth, just done. That's what the Boss Button is for.

## What It Does

The Boss Button immediately cancels the current round:
- All pending meetings are canceled
- Participants are notified automatically
- The round is marked as complete (just... early)

## When to Use It

### Valid Boss Button Moments

- **Company-wide emergency**: All hands needed elsewhere
- **Major outage**: Everyone needs to focus on the fix
- **Holiday week**: Half the team is already out anyway
- **Founder intuition**: Sometimes you just know

### Don't Use It For

- Individual scheduling conflicts (use rescheduling)
- Low participation (review your club settings instead)
- One person's unavailability (they can opt out)

## The Psychology of Authority

The Boss Button works because it's definitive. When leadership makes a clear call, people can adjust immediately without second-guessing.

But with great power... you know the rest. Overuse it and people stop trusting the schedule entirely.

## How to Access It

In your workspace dashboard:
1. Navigate to the active round
2. Click "Boss Button" (it's intentionally prominent)
3. Confirm the cancellation
4. Optional: Add a brief reason

Everyone gets notified within seconds.

## After the Boss Button

Once you hit it, consider:
- When will the next round start?
- Should you adjust the cadence?
- Any feedback to share with participants?

## Trust Your Gut

If you're hesitating about using it, you probably should. The Boss Button exists because sometimes leadership needs to make unilateral decisions for the good of the team.

Use it wisely.
