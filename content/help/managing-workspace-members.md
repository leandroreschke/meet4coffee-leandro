# Managing Workspace Members

Your workspace members are the heart of Meet 4 Coffee. Here's how to invite, manage, and support them.

## Inviting Members

### Invite Links (Recommended)

The easiest way to add team members:

1. Go to **Config > Invites**
2. Click **Create Invite**
3. Choose your settings:
   - **Usage limit**: How many people can use this link
   - **Expires at**: When the link stops working
4. Copy and share the link

Anyone with the link can join instantly after signing in.

## Member Roles

### Owner
- Full control over workspace settings
- Can delete the workspace
- Can manage billing
- Only one owner per workspace

### Member
- Join clubs
- Update their profile
- Opt-out of matchups
- Request reschedules

## Member Status

Members can be in different states:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Invited | Has an invite link but hasn't joined yet |
| Pending Onboarding | Joined but hasn't completed profile |
| Active | Fully set up and participating |
| Inactive | Not currently participating |

## Managing Opt-Outs

Sometimes people shouldn't be matched together. The opt-out feature handles this gracefully.

### Setting Up Opt-Outs

1. Go to **Members**
2. Click on a member's card
3. Click **Manage Opt-Outs**
4. Select people they prefer not to be matched with

### Why Opt-Outs Matter

- **Comfort**: Not everyone clicks with everyone
- **Manager reports**: Some prefer not to chat with their manager casually
n- **Past conflicts**: Discretion preserves team harmony

Opt-outs are private—only workspace owners can see them.

## Member Profiles

Encourage members to complete their profiles:

- **Name**: How they want to be addressed
- **Location**: For timezone awareness
- **Job Title**: Context for conversations
- **Bio**: Conversation starters
- **Interests**: Matching people with shared interests
- **Availability**: When they're free for chats

### Profile Import

Members can import profiles from other workspaces they're in—saves time when joining multiple teams.

## Removing Members

Owners can remove members from the workspace:

1. Go to **Config > Roles**
2. Find the member
3. Click **Remove**

Their data is preserved but they lose access. They can rejoin later with a new invite.

## Best Practices

### Onboarding Flow

1. Send invite link to new hire
2. They join and complete profile
3. Add them to appropriate clubs
4. First round gives them instant team connections

### Regular Cleanup

Review inactive members quarterly. Remove people who've left the company to keep your workspace current.

### Encourage Profile Completion

Empty profiles = awkward conversations. Remind members to fill out:
- A short bio
- A few interests
- Their availability windows

## Troubleshooting

### Member Can't Join

- Check if the invite link expired
- Verify they don't already have an account with that email
- Try creating a new invite link

### Wrong Role Assignment

Only owners can change roles. Members need to contact the workspace owner for role changes.

### Profile Not Saving

- Check all required fields are filled
- Ensure avatar URL is valid (if using a custom photo)
- Try refreshing and re-entering

## Privacy Notes

- Member data is workspace-scoped
- Opt-outs are only visible to owners
- Profile information helps matching but isn't required
